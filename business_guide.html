Apna Business Kyun Shuru Karo?

🚀 Unlimited Income

Job mein salary fixed hoti hai — business mein income ki koi limit nahi. Mehnat se crores tak jaao.

🕐 Time Freedom

Apne time pe kaam karo — koi boss nahi, koi fixed timing nahi. Apni life apne hisab se.

💡 Apni Idea Test Karo

Jo product ya service tumhe pasand hai usse business bana sakte ho — passion se profit.

📋 Business Shuru Karne Ka Sahi Tarika

1
Pehle apna interest aur skill identify karo — passion + skill = best business idea
2
Market research karo — kya log khareed rahe hain? Competition kitna hai?
3
Small scale pe test karo — pehle 10 customers se shuru karo, investment zyada mat lagao
4
Legal formalities: GST registration, MSME certificate, bank account — sab time pe karo
5
Marketing: Word of mouth + WhatsApp + Social media se pehle customers lao
6
Profit reinvest karo business badhao — ek baar momentum aaya toh growth natural hai
